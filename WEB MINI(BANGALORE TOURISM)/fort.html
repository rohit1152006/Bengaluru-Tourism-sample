Bangalore Fort (Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace Area)

★★★★ Built by Kempe Gowda I (1537)

Originally a mud fort, later rebuilt in stone by Haider Ali. Only the Delhi Gate and remnants of the fort wall remain today.

Devanahalli Fort (Near Bengaluru Airport)

★★★ Birthplace of Tipu Sultan

A vast, relatively well-preserved historical fort built of stone, containing many temples and the birthplace of the 'Tiger of Mysore'.

Nandi Fort (Tipu's Drop) (Nandi Hills)

★★★★ Famous for Sunrise Views
